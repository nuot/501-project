Clustering

To further analyze the data, we decided to use a various clustering algorithm to help us identify possible clusters within the theater data set. After trying DBSCAN, K-mean, and Hierarchical clustering method, we decided to use the K-mean cluster for our analysis with K = 10 based on the Silhouette score.
